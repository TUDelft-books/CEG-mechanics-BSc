The shear stress due to torsion in a circular cross section:

$$\tau(r) = \frac{M_tr}{2 \pi R^2 t} = \frac{M_tr}{I_p}$$

This is treated in chapter 6.2.1 of the book Engineering Mechanics Volume 2 {cite:p}`Hartsuijker2007`.
